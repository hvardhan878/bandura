Getting started with Bandura

Everything you need to know

What is Bandura?

Bandura is an AI pre-sales platform for UK IT consulting firms bidding on large government contracts. It automates win probability analysis, competitor matrices, and full capture plans 6-9 months before ITT drops.

How is Bandura different from AutogenAI or mytender.io?

Those tools write first drafts after ITT. Bandura works before the ITT exists. It owns the strategic decision made 6-9 months before the ITT drops: should we bid, how do we win, what do we do now.

What infrastructure do I need?

None. Bandura is built for M365/SharePoint environments. No new infrastructure required. Your bid teams work where they already work.
