About Bandura

Stop reacting to ITTs. Start shaping them.

The problem we solve

Bid teams are always reactive. They only start capture planning when the ITT lands. By then, firms who shaped the opportunity 6 months earlier have already won.

Firms that start capture planning 6 months before ITT win 3x more often. Your competitors are already shaping the deals you'll be reacting to.

The human alternative is a strategic sales consultant at £50K–£200K per engagement. This work either doesn't get done, or it's too expensive to do on every bid. When consultants leave, all that knowledge leaves with them.

Bandura automates win probability analysis, competitor matrices, and full capture plans 6-9 months before ITT drops. No SaaS tool has owned this question before.

We built Bandura for UK IT consulting firms bidding on £50M+ public sector contracts. Trials running on £700M TCV of live deals. One live pilot at £5K MRR. Built for M365/SharePoint environments. No new infrastructure.
